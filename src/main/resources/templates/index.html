Save Our Sons Drop the Guns


We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to eliminating gun violence and helping those who have lost loved ones.

Learn more »

Louisiana is ranked 2nd in America for the highest gun related death rate. According to the Gun Violence Archive, 2020 was a record-breaking year for gun homicides in America. A total of at least 19,223 people lost their lives due to gun violence. Of this number 707 people were Louisiana residents which means thousands of families here at home suffer the grief of loosing someone. In 2019 federal funding was allocated for the study of the prevention of gun violence after a 20 year freeze. While research is greatly needed it will not solve the problem alone. Save Our Sons is dedicated to public awareness, intervention of gun violence and support of the bereaved.
