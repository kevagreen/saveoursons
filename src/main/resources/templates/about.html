About Our Organization

Save Our Sons Drop the Guns is a 501 c(3) Nonprofit Organization founded on July 22, 2018 in memory of Benjamin Finney Jr. Our mission is to raise awareness about gun violence, and the lasting impact that gun violence has on our communities. Our ministry welcomes parents whose child was a victim of gun violence. Save Our Sons Drop The Guns appreciates all donations, and is proud to offer volunteer opportunities, outreach events and other resources that will help further our mission to end gun violence and build a safer community for our youth.
